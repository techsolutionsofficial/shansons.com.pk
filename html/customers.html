CUSTOMERS

It’s our core interest to build long-lasting relationship with customers. Good customer’s relations are just like any relationship, it takes time, mutual interests and willingness to be open and honest to make it work. We try our best to satisfy our customers with fine quality of product perfect as per drawing and material we have agreed upon. Our focus is to deliver parts within given time frame. Here’s a snapshot of some of the brands we are proud to work with, and thanks them for the love and trust they shows on us.

Atlas Honda Limited

Atlas Honda Limited is the largest motorcycle manufacturer in Pakistan with the strongest brand value and highest customer loyalty. The Company is considered a pioneer of motorcycle industry in the country and has been leading two wheeler market successfully for over 50 years.

Millat Equipment Limited

Millat Equipment Limited (MEL) is a part of Millat Group, comprising of four other companies. Millat Equipment Limited is one of the leading manufacturing organizations in Pakistan producing high quality transmission gears. The company acquired Gear manufacturing setup from M/S ACGO LIMITED – UK

Brother Engineering Industries

Brother Engineering Industries is in automobile industry of Pakistan from more than 40 years. Journey of the organization was commenced in 1974. The company is one of the pioneer vendors in Pakistan Automobile Industry.

Pakistan Springs & Engineering Co. (Pvt.) Ltd.

Pakistan Springs & Engineering Co. (Pvt.) Ltd is specialized in manufacturing of springs, machining parts, bike gear shift levers and bike exhaust muffler. Company also has following automatic / manual Zinc Plant, Nickel Plant, Heat treatment Plant and Gas Carburizing as well.
