DEPARTMENTS

In our organization following departments are working.

Accounts & HR

The Human Resources and Accounting Coordinator is responsible for supporting Human Resource services, primarily in benefits administration, recruiting, new employee on boarding and a variety of general administrative Human Resources tasks. During peak season this Coordinator will assist the finance department with administrative tasks as well.

Production

Most of our valuable products and parts are developed in production area. This is the back-bone of our facility.


 

CNC (Computer Numerical Control)

Some of our parts are being manufactured in CNC department. We have stat-of-the-art CNC machines and qualified CNC machine operators.

 

FGS (Finished Goods Store)

This is the area where all our finished products are checked and packed. All our finished products are available here.

 

Main Store

Our main department of overall keeping all the products and raw materials etc. This department keeps all the incoming and outgoing material and products records.
 

Quality Assurance & Quality Control

We have stat-of-the-art machines including hardness testing machines, mounting machines and heat treatment apparatus. Our quality is our top priority.
 

Security

Security of our facility is carried out by our passionate and hard-working security guards. They are hardworking and loyal to their job

.

Dispatch

Our dispatch area delivers items to our valuable customers by verifying orders. It manages our inventories stock and arranges arranges transportation.
